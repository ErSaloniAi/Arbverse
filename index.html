The Problem

Why Ethereum Needed Layer 2

Ethereum's success led to congestion, high fees, and slow transactions. Layer 2 solutions emerged as the answer.

Slow Transactions

Ethereum processes only 15-30 transactions per second, causing delays during high network activity.

High Gas Fees

During peak times, a simple token swap could cost $50-$200 in gas fees, pricing out everyday users.

Network Congestion

Popular NFT drops and DeFi events would clog the network, affecting all users regardless of their activity.

Layer 2 Solution

Layer 2 networks process transactions off-chain while inheriting Ethereum's security, solving all three problems.

The Solution

What is Arbitrum?

Arbitrum is a Layer 2 scaling solution built on top of Ethereum. It uses Optimistic Rollup technology to bundle hundreds of transactions together and process them off the main Ethereum chain.

By moving computation off-chain while posting transaction data to Ethereum, Arbitrum achieves massive scalability without sacrificing the security and decentralization that makes Ethereum valuable.
